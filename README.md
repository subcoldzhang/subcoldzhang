## About Me

I am Yuhao Zhang, currently working at Li Auto as an Algorithm Engineer, where I focus on AI algorithm research and development, solution design, and model building for production systems.

Before joining Li Auto, I worked in a lab in the United States on AI for drug discovery under the supervision of Zhaoqian Su. During that period, I was also actively exploring quantitative trading and related research.
